Scores

This game is a HTML5 reimplementation of a open source DOS classic. As of July 2015, it is still in development.
Maybe you would like to take a look at the Source code?

Controls:

  • M: toggle sound
  • P: toggle pause
  • 1-4: toggle player 1-4
  • Alt + 1-4: toggle AI for player 1-4
  • Arrows: control player 1
  • WAD: control player 2
  • JIL: control player 3
  • NumPad: control player 4
