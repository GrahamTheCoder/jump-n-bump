Jump'n'Bump - HTML5

This game is a HTML5 reimplementation of a DOS classic. As of October 2010, it is still in development.
Maybe you would like to take a look at the Source code?



Debug:

Keyboard map:

  • M: toggle sound
  • 1-4: toggle player 1-4 (active, AI, none)
  • Arrows: control player 1
  • WAD: control player 2
  • JIL: control player 3
  • NumPad: control player 4
